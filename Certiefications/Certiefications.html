Certifications

Our certifications are a true testimony of the belief entrusted by various Indian and International agencies in us.

Inspection agencies like Lloyds Registrar of Shipping, American Bureau of Shipping, Det Norske Veritas etc. have

approved our plants and products.

Re-emphasizing our focus on Quality, we involve employees at all level and hold periodic reviews of our Quality

Policies and Objectives for their suitability and effectiveness with one clear goal in mind – ‘Client Satisfaction’.
